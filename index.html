Hirevo is the direct hire platform built for companies in Netherlands and Germany that want to hire verified EU workers without paying recruitment agency fees. Companies browse all worker profiles for free and pay only a small fee to unlock the contact details of workers they want to hire directly.

Whether you are hiring permanent staff or seasonal workers, the Hirevo direct hire platform gives you access to verified EU candidates from Romania, Poland, Bulgaria, Hungary, Czech Republic, Slovakia, Croatia, Lithuania, Latvia and Estonia.

How the Direct Hire Platform Works

The Hirevo direct hire platform operates in three simple steps. First, workers from EU countries register a free profile on hirevo.nl with their work experience, skills, language level, availability and preferred destination country in Netherlands or Germany. Second, companies in Netherlands and Germany browse all verified worker profiles on the direct hire platform at no cost using filters for sector, location, employment type and availability date. Third, when a company identifies a suitable candidate on the direct hire platform, they pay to unlock the contact details and reach out directly. No agency, no recruiter, no intermediary involved at any stage.

Frequently Asked Questions About the Direct Hire Platform

Is the direct hire platform free for workers? Yes. Worker profiles on the Hirevo direct hire platform are completely free forever. No registration fees, no placement fees, no agency commissions.

How is Hirevo different from a recruitment agency? Hirevo is a direct hire platform, not an agency. There are no agency fees, no per-hire commissions and no middlemen. Companies on the direct hire platform contact workers directly and negotiate employment terms without any agency involved.

Which sectors are available on the direct hire platform? The Hirevo direct hire platform covers logistics, warehouse, manufacturing, hospitality, construction, agriculture and cleaning across Netherlands and Germany.

Is the direct hire platform GDPR compliant? Yes. All data on the Hirevo direct hire platform is stored in the European Union and users can delete their account at any time from the platform settings.

Can workers from outside the EU use the direct hire platform? Currently the Hirevo direct hire platform serves EU citizens only, as Netherlands and Germany require EU freedom of movement documentation for blue collar employment.
