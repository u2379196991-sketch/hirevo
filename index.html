Hirevo — Direct Hire Platform for Netherlands and Germany

Hirevo is a B2B direct hiring marketplace that connects verified EU workers with companies in the Netherlands and Germany. No recruitment agency fees, no per-hire commissions, no middlemen. Workers create free profiles forever and companies contact them directly.

Hire Workers in Netherlands and Germany Without Agency Fees

Companies in the Netherlands and Germany can browse all verified worker profiles for free on Hirevo. Filter by sector, location, employment type and availability. Pay only when you unlock a worker contact details. No agency markup, no hidden fees, no long-term contracts required.

Available Sectors

Hirevo covers the following sectors in the Netherlands and Germany: logistics and warehouse operations, manufacturing and production, hospitality and hotel services, construction and civil works, greenhouse agriculture and seasonal harvesting, cleaning and facility management.

How Hirevo Works

Workers register a free profile with their experience, skills, languages and availability. Companies browse all profiles at no cost. When a company wants to contact a worker, they pay a small fee to unlock the contact details. No agency is involved at any stage. All contracts are signed directly between the worker and the company.

Why Choose Hirevo Over a Recruitment Agency

Traditional recruitment agencies in the Netherlands charge 15 to 30 percent of annual salary per placement. Hirevo eliminates this cost entirely. Companies pay only for the contacts they actually want to pursue. Workers receive 100 percent of their salary with no deductions for placement fees.
